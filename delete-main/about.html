Our Story

Established in 2023, Sungroupscontractor has quickly become a trusted name in the construction industry. We specialize in delivering reliable workmanship across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.

Our mission is to deliver exceptional quality, innovative solutions, and unmatched customer service in every project we undertake. From epoxy floors to complex electrical and boiler systems, our certified experts ensure safety and performance.
